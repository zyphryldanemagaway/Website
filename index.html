What Makes Filipino Food Special?

Filipino cuisine is a unique blend of sweet, salty, and sour flavors, influenced by Malay, Spanish, Chinese, and American cultures. Each dish reflects the warmth and creativity of Filipino cooking.

Adobo

A savory stew made with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and pepper.

Sinigang

A comforting sour soup made with tamarind, vegetables, and meat or seafood.

Lechon

Whole roasted pork with crispy skin, often served during fiestas.

Halo-Halo

A refreshing dessert of shaved ice, sweet beans, fruits, and ube.
